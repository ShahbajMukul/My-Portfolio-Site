Extracurricular Activities

Developers of Dearborn

As an active member of the Developers of Dearborn Club at the University of Michigan-Dearborn, I engage in collaborative game development projects, contributing to both front-end and back-end aspects. In our current school-year long project, we are crafting an immersive story-driven 2D game that plunges players into a world of investigation and mystery. The gameplay intertwines running, combat, and dialogue, offering a rich interactive experience. Players make choices that influence the narrative, leading to multiple endings. This approach ensures a unique journey for each player, as their decisions shape the game's conclusion. Our team is dedicated to delivering engaging gameplay mechanics and a compelling story that keeps players on the edge of their seats, eagerly anticipating what their choices will bring next.
